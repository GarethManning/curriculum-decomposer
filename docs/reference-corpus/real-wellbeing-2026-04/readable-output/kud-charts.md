# REAL School Budapest — KUD by Competency by Band

## Emotional Intelligence
*The ability to understand, express, and manage one's emotions while empathetically responding to others.*

### Band A — Water/Air Dragons (ages 5–8)

**Self-Awareness & Regulation**

- · [Do-Disposition] I can notice what my body is telling me
- · [Do-Skill] I can name a feeling
- · [Do-Skill] I can use one calming strategy to return to learning

**Social Awareness & Empathy**

- · [Do-Skill] I can name a feeling another person might be having
- · [Do-Disposition] I offer kind words to others when they have feelings
- · [Do-Disposition] I offer specific helpful actions to others when they have feelings

### Band B — Earth Dragons (ages 8–10)

**Self-Awareness & Regulation**

- · [Do-Disposition] I can identify a common personal trigger
- · [Do-Skill] I can choose a practised strategy to manage my response

**Social Awareness & Empathy**

- · [Do-Skill] I can describe a situation from another person's perspective
- · [Do-Disposition] I ask if someone would like help before acting to assist them

### Band C — Fire Dragons (ages 10–12)

**Self-Awareness & Regulation**

- · [Do-Disposition] I can apply a chosen strategy in a challenging situation
- · [Do-Skill] I can reflect on how well a strategy worked

**Social Awareness & Empathy**

- · [Do-Disposition] I notice what others say and do in a group
- · [Do-Disposition] I check in when I'm unsure about others' needs or boundaries
- · [Do-Disposition] I adjust my actions to match others' stated needs or boundaries

### Band D — Metal/Light Dragons (ages 12–14)

**Self-Awareness & Regulation**

- · [Do-Disposition] I can apply my strategies under stress across different settings
- · [Do-Skill] I can explain what worked and what didn't in my strategy use
- · [Do-Skill] I can state one change for next time based on my reflection

**Social Awareness & Empathy**

- · [Do-Disposition] I repair relationships after conflict by listening to the other person
- · [Do-Disposition] I own my part in conflicts when repairing relationships
- · [Do-Skill] I can agree on a next step that works for both people after conflict

## Metacognitive Self-Direction
*The ability to notice, analyse, and intentionally adjust one's own thinking and learning patterns.*

### Band A — Water/Air Dragons (ages 5–8)

**Pattern Analysis & Adjustment**

- · [Do-Skill] I can describe one thing I noticed about how I reacted to a situation
- · [Do-Skill] I can say what I might do differently next time

**Self-Direction in Practice**

- · [Do-Disposition] Stops and tries a different approach when the first attempt does not work, without being told to
- · [Do-Disposition] After a setback, names what happened rather than dismissing it
- · [Do-Disposition] Accepts a suggestion to try differently without extended resistance

### Band B — Earth Dragons (ages 8–10)

**Pattern Analysis & Adjustment**

- · [Do-Skill] I can identify a pattern in how I tend to respond in a type of situation
- · [Do-Skill] I can give a reason for why that pattern might happen

**Self-Direction in Practice**

- · [Do-Disposition] Refers back to a strategy they have previously found helpful, without being prompted
- · [Do-Disposition] After a task, volunteers one specific observation about how they worked
- · [Do-Disposition] When something goes wrong, asks a question about why rather than placing blame

### Band C — Fire Dragons (ages 10–12)

**Pattern Analysis & Adjustment**

- · [Do-Skill] I can analyse a pattern across more than one situation
- · [Do-Skill] I can explain what drives a pattern
- · [Do-Skill] I can describe a specific adjustment I have made or will make

**Self-Direction in Practice**

- · [Do-Disposition] Identifies a pattern in how they tend to respond across more than one situation and names it spontaneously in conversation
- · [Do-Disposition] Adjusts their approach mid-task in response to noticing something is not working
- · [Do-Disposition] In reflective writing, connects a current response to a pattern they have identified previously

### Band D — Metal/Light Dragons (ages 12–14)

**Pattern Analysis & Adjustment**

- · [Do-Skill] I can evaluate a pattern in my thinking or responses across different contexts
- · [Do-Skill] I can justify what sustains a pattern
- · [Do-Skill] I can describe the effect of a specific adjustment I have made

**Self-Direction in Practice**

- · [Do-Disposition] Articulates a specific adjustment they have made to their thinking or behaviour and describes its effect across more than one domain
- · [Do-Disposition] In novel or challenging situations, applies a self-awareness strategy without an adult prompt
- · [Do-Disposition] Makes connections between wellbeing learning and behaviour in other contexts, unprompted
